## Data Processing Overview

We process personal data only when necessary for the operation of our website, or based on your consent or legitimate interest.

### Server Log Files
When visiting our site, technical data (IP address, browser, timestamp) is automatically collected for security and stability.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate interest).

### Contact Forms
If you use our contact forms, we process the data you provide (name, email, message) to handle your request.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (Contract/Pre-contractual) or Art. 6(1)(a) (Consent).

### Cookies and Analytics (Google Analytics 4)
We use Google Analytics 4 to analyze website usage. This service uses cookies and processes data (including truncated IPs) to provide us with anonymous statistics.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (Consent). You can revoke this via our Cookie Settings.

### External Media (YouTube)
We embed videos from YouTube. Connection to YouTube servers is established only if you consent to "Media".
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (Consent).

### OpenStreetMap
We use OpenStreetMap to display maps. This is necessary to show you our location. Your IP address may be transmitted to OpenStreetMap Foundation for this purpose.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate interest).
